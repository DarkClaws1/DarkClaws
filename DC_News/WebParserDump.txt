										<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>A truck driver in China has gone viral for his three rescue cats who ride around the country with him while he works. </p>
<p>According to the <a href="https://www.scmp.com/news/people-culture/trending-china/article/3204127/they-sleep-mini-bunk-bed-truck-drivers-adventures-road-china-3-stray-cats-charm-social-media" rel="noopener" target="_blank">South China Morning Post</a>, the 56-year-old long-distance truck driver named Wang adopted the three cats after finding them as strays while working on the road. </p>
<figure id="attachment_425434" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425434" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111347.570.png" alt="" width="1000" height="813" class="size-full wp-image-425434" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111347.570.png 1000w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111347.570-416x338.png 416w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111347.570-830x675.png 830w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111347.570-208x169.png 208w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111347.570-768x624.png 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425434" class="wp-caption-text">Photo: <a href="https://www.facebook.com/watch/?v=1000530977757989" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Facebook/9GAG</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>That was back in 2020, and the cats haven&#8217;t left his side since! </p>
<p>As shared in a video by <a href="https://www.facebook.com/watch/?v=1000530977757989" rel="noopener" target="_blank">9GAG</a>, Wang didn&#8217;t just adopt the cats and let them hang out in the passenger seat. He created them an entire bunk bed to relax on! </p>
<figure id="attachment_425435" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425435" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111437.853.png" alt="" width="1000" height="813" class="size-full wp-image-425435" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111437.853.png 1000w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111437.853-416x338.png 416w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111437.853-830x675.png 830w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111437.853-208x169.png 208w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111437.853-768x624.png 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425435" class="wp-caption-text">Photo: <a href="https://www.facebook.com/watch/?v=1000530977757989" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Facebook/9GAG</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>The three-tier bunk bed features a comfy cat haven for each rescue feline to sleep in while they drive. On stops, the cats get out of the truck and stretch their legs. They use the restroom outside and come back to the truck when it&#8217;s time to keep going. </p>
<p>It may be unconventional, but it&#8217;s pretty incredible, and the cats seem so happy! </p>
<figure id="attachment_425436" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425436" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111532.949.png" alt="" width="1000" height="813" class="size-full wp-image-425436" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111532.949.png 1000w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111532.949-416x338.png 416w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111532.949-830x675.png 830w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111532.949-208x169.png 208w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Untitled-design-2023-07-21T111532.949-768x624.png 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425436" class="wp-caption-text">Photo: <a href="https://www.facebook.com/watch/?v=1000530977757989" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Facebook/9GAG</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>Life as a stray cat is tough, and seeing these three cats have the care and companionship they need to thrive is truly something special. Plus, the cats offer Wang some company on the road while he&#8217;s away from home. </p>

										<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>A sweet little dog discovered in a Texas cornfield has been rescued from a life of pain and misery after who knows how long when the South Plains Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA) stepped in and came to his aid. </p>
<figure id="attachment_425419" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425419" style="width: 1170px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-2.jpeg" alt="matted dog" width="1170" height="1367" class="size-full wp-image-425419" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-2.jpeg 1170w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-2-289x338.jpeg 289w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-2-578x675.jpeg 578w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-2-145x169.jpeg 145w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-2-768x897.jpeg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425419" class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo: Facebook/South Plains SPCA</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>&#8220;When I first got the call, these people had found this dog behind their house in a cornfield, and I got a call by my president [Kim Moyers] to go pick this dog up,&#8221; Tori Houston, a foster mom and volunteer with the South Plains SPCA, told <em><a href="https://people.com/matted-texas-rescue-dog-gets-makeover-removes-pounds-of-fur-7563638" rel="noopener" target="_blank">PEOPLE</a></em>. </p>
<figure id="attachment_425417" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425417" style="width: 1170px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-1.jpeg" alt="Matted dog" width="1170" height="1193" class="size-full wp-image-425417" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-1.jpeg 1170w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-1-331x338.jpeg 331w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-1-662x675.jpeg 662w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-1-166x169.jpeg 166w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-dog-1-768x783.jpeg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425417" class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo: Facebook/South Plains SPCA</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>&#8220;And when I arrived, the condition of this dog was probably the worst I had ever seen in any dog, ever. He had so much hair you didn&#8217;t know which was the front, which was the back. He was covered in goat heads (stickers). He stunk so bad,&#8221; she noted. &#8220;You couldn&#8217;t see any part of his body. He looked like a pile of sheep wool.&#8221;</p>
<p>Referring to him as Matt now (for obvious reasons), Moyers went on to say that his hair growth was about five years of matting!</p>
<figure id="attachment_425423" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425423" style="width: 1006px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-3.0.jpg" alt="matted dog being groomed" width="1006" height="1193" class="size-full wp-image-425423" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-3.0.jpg 1006w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-3.0-285x338.jpg 285w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-3.0-569x675.jpg 569w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-3.0-143x169.jpg 143w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-3.0-768x911.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425423" class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo: Facebook/South Plains SPCA</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>According to the <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca/posts/pfbid02YpWcDUpcgw9XBe8Z2grqTifb3WX38fnoQVBxu52oZqK29eQGbccsYEV6yZrhYbZBl" rel="noopener" target="_blank">South Plains SPCA</a>, &#8220;Matt went to Ark Hospital for Pets around 7:15 Monday morning with his foster, Tori Houston. Kayla Thurmon, with Kayla&#8217;s Grooming Service, came to the vet office, and after Matt was sedated, Kayla performed her grooming magic! Matt&#8217;s total hair shaved off weighed in around 3 lbs. There were thousands of goat head stickers, sticks, grass, and even beetles matted in his fur. </p>
<figure id="attachment_425424" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425424" style="width: 999px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-fur-on-floor-1.0.jpg" alt="Matted fur" width="999" height="1201" class="size-full wp-image-425424" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-fur-on-floor-1.0.jpg 999w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-fur-on-floor-1.0-281x338.jpg 281w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-fur-on-floor-1.0-561x675.jpg 561w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-fur-on-floor-1.0-141x169.jpg 141w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/matted-fur-on-floor-1.0-768x923.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425424" class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo: Facebook/South Plains SPCA</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>&#8220;He has severe bruises on his body from his fur being tight on his skin. Blood work has been done, and he is anemic and on medication. He is heartworm negative and tick-disease-negative. There is NO way ticks and fleas could have crawled through his matted fur. Matt will need a dental once his blood work indicates it&#8217;s safe. He is also on eye drops several times a day, as his eyes are extremely irritated. After his groom and him waking up, he is such a sweet boy. His age is estimated 6 years old.  His care will be lengthy, but he will be up for adoption once he has healed and all vetting done.&#8221;</p>
<figure id="attachment_425425" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425425" style="width: 1008px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-2.0.jpg" alt="rescue dog after being groomed" width="1008" height="1190" class="size-full wp-image-425425" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-2.0.jpg 1008w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-2.0-286x338.jpg 286w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-2.0-572x675.jpg 572w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-2.0-143x169.jpg 143w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-2.0-768x907.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425425" class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo: Facebook/South Plains SPCA</a></figcaption></figure>
<p>Prior to his grooming, one of their Facebook posts also noted that &#8220;there was a leash embedded throughout the hair and that led to a collar embedded to the neck. We were able to cut the collar/leash out in segments. If you can, please donate towards the care of this dog.&#8221; They also added &#8220;You can call Ark Hospital for Pets between 7:00 am &#8211; 1 pm to donate (806-745-2955), donate via Venmo, or donations can be brought by our facility.&#8221;</p>
<figure id="attachment_425426" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-425426" style="width: 1039px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-1.0.jpg" alt="adoptable dog" width="1039" height="1155" class="size-full wp-image-425426" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-1.0.jpg 1039w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-1.0-304x338.jpg 304w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-1.0-607x675.jpg 607w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-1.0-152x169.jpg 152w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Matt-1.0-768x854.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-425426" class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://www.facebook.com/SouthPlainsSpca" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo: Facebook/South Plains SPCA</a></figcaption></figure>
