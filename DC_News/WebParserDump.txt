										<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>Losing an ability is absolutely terrifying ‚Äî you&#8217;ll never know if you&#8217;ll learn to do it again. It&#8217;s scarier if it&#8217;s the ability to move a part of your body that is essential to your daily life. One of the most terrifying conditions would be leg paralysis. Not being able to walk can negatively impact someone ‚Äî especially their mental health. This will raise a lot of questions, and it can make someone feel inadequate.</p>
<figure id="attachment_389686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-389686" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-1.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="1000" class="size-full wp-image-389686" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-1.jpg 1000w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-1-338x338.jpg 338w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-1-675x675.jpg 675w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-1-169x169.jpg 169w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-1-768x768.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-389686" class="wp-caption-text">Photo: Youtube/The Dodo</figcaption></figure>
<p>For this reason, a support system is vital during the recovery stage. You&#8217;ll need people who will encourage and aid you in overcoming negative thoughts that can hugely affect the healing process. Sadly, even animals can experience leg paralysis. Lucky for this rooster, he has someone who supported him throughout his recovery. Napoleon is a rooster from the Alpaca Treehouse in Atlanta ‚Äî which is being managed by his human friend, Kara.</p>
<p>&#8220;Napoleon landed in a terrible situation. He was hung upside down, and he lost movement in both of his legs,&#8221; Kara explained. &#8220;At first, he was just a feathered lump in a towel donut. Neither leg was functioning at all.&#8221; They had to consult a vet to further understand Napoleon&#8217;s condition. According to the vet, Napoleon&#8217;s legs weren&#8217;t entirely numb, which sparked hope for Kara. From day one, the Alpaca Treehouse owner ensured that Napoleon was given extra care. She became the rooster&#8217;s companion ‚Äî which was extremely helpful for him.</p>
<figure id="attachment_389687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-389687" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-2.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="523" class="size-full wp-image-389687" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-2.jpg 1000w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-2-600x314.jpg 600w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-2-300x157.jpg 300w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-2-768x402.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-389687" class="wp-caption-text">Photo: Youtube/The Dodo</figcaption></figure>
<p>After a week, Napoleon just stayed in one place but slowly returned to his livelier self. Kara took it as a good sign of recovery then they started with physical therapy. Napoleon was so cooperative during each therapy session ‚Äî his determination was apparent. Seeing him gradually learn how to walk again without any fuss was wonderful. He was listening to everything the therapist instructed him to do.</p>
<figure id="attachment_389688" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-389688" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img loading="lazy" src="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-3.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="1000" class="size-full wp-image-389688" data-srcset="https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-3.jpg 1000w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-3-338x338.jpg 338w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-3-675x675.jpg 675w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-3-169x169.jpg 169w, https://d1dd4ethwnlwo2.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/rooster-walk-in-line-3-768x768.jpg 768w" data-sizes="(min-width:1200px) 1200px, (min-width:992px) 992px, (min-width:768px) 768px, (min-width:576px) 480px, 300px" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-389688" class="wp-caption-text">Photo: Youtube/The Dodo</figcaption></figure>
<p>&#8220;We would do these mini sessions a couple times a day. I had no idea what the outcome would be, but we never gave up on him, and he never gave up on himself,&#8221; Kara said. Due to consistent therapy and Napoleon&#8217;s perseverance, he was able to stand up on his own one day. It was a win for him and Kara ‚Äî their hope became more vibrant. Eventually, the mighty rooster was able to join his friends back in the coop. He showed tremendous improvement every day. To be accompanied by a great person like Kara and being a strong-willed rooster, there is no doubt that walking again is possible.</p>
<p>Napoleon went from standing up with his legs to running around the coop again. His story is one for the books which can serve as an inspiration to anyone feeling hopeless at the moment. The Alpaca Treehouse was the right place for Napoleon, where he had a successful healing process. You can visit and learn more about their place by following them on their socials. They are available on <a href="https://www.instagram.com/alpacatreehouse/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Instagram</a> and <a href="https://www.tiktok.com/@alpacatreehouse" rel="noopener" target="_blank">TikTok</a>. </p>

<p>The Queen&#8217;s death comes after she had stepped back from many events in recent months due to mobility issues. She had also lost her husband of 73 years, Prince Philip, the Duke of Edinburgh, last year.</p>
<p>Queen Elizabeth II was born April 21, 1926 in London to Prince Albert, Duke of York, and Lady Elizabeth Bowes-Lyon. Her father was the second son of the reigning king, George V, but when his older brother, Edward VIII, abdicated in 1936, the young princess became heir to the throne.</p>
<p>She began her life of service to her country at a young age, <a href="https://www.nationalww2museum.org/war/articles/queen-elizabeth-ii-during-world-war-ii">serving in</a> the Auxiliary Territorial Service of the British Army during World War II. <p>So what comes next? The protocol is contained in &#8220;Operation London Bridge.&#8221;</p>
<p>When a monarch dies, a code has often been used to communicate the fact. In Queen Elizabeth&#8217;s case, that code was &#8220;London Bridge is down.&#8221; Once that code was relayed to the prime minister, the news was shared with the governments of the Commonwealth nations and the nations outside the UK for which the Queen serves as head of state. After that, the public learned the news.</p>
<p>That was accomplished in two primary ways: A footman dressed in black pinned the death announcement to Buckingham Palace&#8217;s gates, and a news alert was sent to global media simultaneously. The palace also communicated the message on its website, which reverted to a single page with a dark background.</p>
<p>With flags lowered to honor the Queen on the day of her death, they will be raised the next morning. At that time, Charles III will officially be named King and his wife Camilla will officially become Queen Consort. The new monarch will then embark on a tour of the UK.</p>
<p>Meanwhile, the Queen will lie in state at Westminster Hall ahead of her funeral, which will be held on the 10th day after her death. This occasion will be marked with a national day of mourning. She will be buried in St. George&#8217;s Chapel at Windsor Castle in the King George VI Memorial Chapel, with her parents, sister Princess Margaret, and husband Prince Philip.</p>
